sprite cake

description

sure, this was a 7up cake recipe, but i prefer sprite!

ingredients

cake

  • all-purpose flour (3 cups)
  • granulated sugar (3 cups)
  • unsalted butter (1.5 cups)
  • sprite (0.5 cups)
  • lemon juice (0.25 cups)
  • eggs (5 large)

glaze

  • powdered sugar (3.25 cups)
  • sprite (0.25 cups)
  • lemon juice (3 tbsp)
  • vanilla extract (1 tsp)

steps

  1. preheat oven to 325°f
  2. grease a bundt pan with butter or oil
  3. using a mixer on medium-high, beat the butter and sugar until smooth and creamy
  4. mix in the eggs and lemon juice
  5. mix in the flour
  6. mix in the sprite for 1 minute
  7. pour batter into the pan and bake for 1 hour or until a toothpick in the center comes out clean
  8. let the cake cool in the pan for at least 30 minutes
  9. for the glaze, beat the powdered sugar, vanilla, lemon juice, and sprite on medium speed until smooth
  10. drizzle the glaze over the cake and enjoy!
