rum cake

description

rum.. cake.. what more could i ask for??

ingredients

cake

  • all-purpose flour (2 cups)
  • granulated sugar (1.5 cups)
  • vegetable oil (0.5 cup)
  • milk (0.5 cups)
  • rum (0.5 cups)
  • unsalted butter (1 stick)
  • eggs (4 large)
  • vanilla instant pudding mix (3.4oz box)
  • baking powder (2 tsp)
  • vanilla extract (2 tsp)
  • table salt (1 tsp)

syrup

  • granulated sugar (1 cup)
  • rum (0.75 cups)
  • unsalted butter (1 stick)
  • vanilla extract (0.5 tsp)
  • table salt (0.25 tsp)

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 325°f
  2. mix the flour, sugar, pudding mix, baking powder, salt, butter, and vegetable oil at medium speed until everything is combined
  3. mix in the milk, then the eggs one at a time
  4. mix in the rum and vanilla
  5. grease the pan with butter or oil spray then coat it with flour
  6. pour the batter into the pan and spread it evenly
  7. bake for 50 to 60 minutes or until a long toothpick comes out clean after being inserted
  8. while the cake cools, combine the syrup ingredients except for the vanilla in a saucepan
  9. bring to a rapid boil then simmer without stirring for 5 to 8 minutes or until the syrup thickens slightly
  10. remove from the heat and stir in the vanilla
  11. use a long skewer to poke holes all over the cake
  12. pour the syrup over the cake in .25 cup batches until it is all soaked up
  13. cover the pan loosely with plastic wrap and leave overnight at room temperature
  14. when ready to serve, loosen the edges of the cake and turn it onto a serving plate. if it doesn't release, place it in the oven and set it to 350°f for 10 minutes then try again
  15. enjoy the fruits of your labor!
